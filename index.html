About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Service Science and Operations Management, School of Management, Zhejiang University. I obtain my Ph.D. from Department of Management Sciences, College of Business, City University of Hong Kong, where I am supervised by Prof. Frank Chen. Before that, I received the B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics from School of Information, Renmin Univsersity of China.

Research Interests

  • Methodologies: Dynamic Mechanism Design, Information Design, Optimal Control, Dynamic Programming, Machine Learning

  • Applications: Incentives Design in Healthcare Operation Management, Supply Chain Management, Pricing and Revenue Management, Blockchain and Smart Contract Applications, Interface between Econ, Marketing and OM
