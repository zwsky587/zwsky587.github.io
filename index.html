Publications and Working Papers

  • The Strategic Role of Supplier Learning, Long Gao, Mohammad E. Nikoofal, Wei Zhang ,Accepted at Manufacturing & Service Operations Management, 2022.
    Idea: We study a procurement problem, where the supplier holds superior cost information and can learn to improve efficiency over time.

  • Dynamic Incentives in Contract Farming, Wei Zhang, Long Gao, Mohammad Zolghadr, Dawei Jian, Mohsen, Elhafsi, Minor Revision at Production and Operation Management, 2022.
